# How to move a paid team to a organization?

#### Q:  Can I move a paid team into a **paid organization?**
A: No, paid teams cannot be directly moved into a paid organization due to billing conflicts. You’ll need to downgrade the team to the free plan first and then  you will have the opportunity to move your team to organization.

#### Q:  How do I proceed if I want to move my paid team into a paid organization?
A:  **Downgrade the team to the free plan** (Required cancellation of the current subscription)
-   Contact to Apidog support to request downgrade and pro-related refund for unused time.
-   Once downgraded, you can move the team into the organization.

#### Q: What information do I need to provide to support to downgrade teams plan?
- **Team ID**(e.g, `123456`)
- **Organization ID** (e.g., `123456`)
- Preferred resolution (Downgrade + Refund / billing consolidation)


#### Q: Will I get refund if I downgrade?
A: Depending on the cycle and other terms, Apidog will issue refund based on unused time.


#### Q: What will with the data if I downgrade?
A: When you downgrade/unsubscibe from a paid plan, your existing data (APIs, projects, etc.) will remain intact, but some features may become restricted based on your new plan.

#### Q: Can I transfer a paid team to a **free Organization?** 

A: Yes, you can transfer your **one** paid team to a free organization and the plan status of your current organization will be updated according to the plan of the transferred team.

#### Q: What role should one have to transfer the paid team to organization?

A: One should be the owner of both of the teams and organization to do the transfer operation.

#### Q: Should I backup my data before downgrading?
A: It’s recommended to check your current usage and back up critical data if you’re unsure about the new plan’s restrictions.

#### Q: Can I do this myself, or do I need support?
A: You can transfer a free team to a paid organization or a paid team to a free organization, but only if you are the owner of both. Downgrading may require manual handling—if needed, email APIDOG Support with the required details.  Contact us here - [Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md)
